## 1. Our Commitment to Safety

{{PRODUCT_NAME}} is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and legal environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for illegal activity, exploitation, harassment, or abuse.

## 2. Prohibited Content & Behavior

Users must NOT post, share, or promote:

### 2.1 Illegal Content

- Illegal drugs or controlled substances
- Weapons or explosives (except educational context)
- Human trafficking or exploitation
- Stolen goods or fraud
- Hacking or unauthorized access tools
- Counterfeit goods or currency
- Any content that violates law

### 2.2 Violence & Harm

- Instructions for violence or self-harm
- Graphic violence or gore
- Threats, harassment, or bullying
- Glorification of violence or terrorism
- Incitement to violence
- Non-consensual violence imagery

### 2.3 Exploitation & Abuse

- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) — reported to NCMEC
- Child grooming or endangerment
- Human trafficking
- Sex trafficking
- Domestic abuse or coercion
- Non-consensual intimate images
- Deepfake intimate content without consent

### 2.4 Harassment & Bullying

- Targeted harassment of individuals or groups
- Hate speech or discrimination (race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, etc.)
- Doxxing (sharing private information)
- Swatting or false reports to authorities
- Coordinated abuse ("pile-on" attacks)
- Dead-naming or misgendering

### 2.5 Misinformation & Fraud

- Deliberate misinformation intended to deceive
- Impersonation of others
- Phishing or social engineering
- Scams or financial fraud
- False medical/health claims
- Misleading product endorsements
- Election misinformation (depending on jurisdiction)

### 2.6 Intellectual Property Violations

- Unlicensed copyrighted content (music, movies, images)
- Trademark or brand infringement
- Counterfeited software or licenses
- Plagiarism
- Trade secret theft

### 2.7 Spam & Manipulation

- Commercial spam or advertising
- Bot networks or artificial engagement
- Coordinated inauthentic behavior
- Repetitive unwanted contact
- Manipulation of platform features

## 4. Enforcement & Consequences

### 4.1 Violation Levels

**Level 1: Warning**
- Minor violations (first offense)
- Examples: Spam, minor harassment, policy clarification needed
- Action: Warning + education

**Level 2: Suspension**
- Moderate violations or repeat Level 1 violations
- Duration: 24 hours to 30 days
- Examples: Coordinated harassment, hate speech, repeated spam

**Level 3: Permanent Ban**
- Severe violations or repeat Level 2 violations
- Examples: CSAM, threats, exploitation, coordinated abuse
- Action: Permanent account deletion + possible law enforcement report

### 4.2 Law Enforcement Reporting

We report to authorities:
- Child sexual abuse material (NCMEC)
- Threats of violence or terrorism (FBI, local law enforcement)
- Human trafficking (NCMEC, local authorities)
- Fraud or financial crimes

## 5. Reporting & Moderation

### 5.1 How to Report Violations

1. Click "Report" on user profile or content
2. Select violation type and provide details
3. Attach evidence if available
4. Submit — we review within 24-48 hours

**Report Email:** {{CONTACT_EMAIL}}
**Report Form:** {{REPORT_FORM_URL}}

### 5.2 Our Moderation Process

1. **Intake:** Report logged and assigned to team
2. **Investigation:** Reviewed by human moderators
3. **Decision:** Violations confirmed or dismissed
4. **Action:** Applied based on violation level
5. **Appeal:** Users may appeal within 30 days

### 5.3 Appeal Process

1. Go to Settings > Support > Moderation Appeal
2. Explain why the decision was incorrect
3. Provide additional evidence
4. Independent reviewer re-examines case

**Appeal deadline:** 30 days from action date
